GALLERY

Nature has an important part in everybody’s life, to make such practical knowledge accessible to rural children , this programme was started on 2nd December 2021. Workers from our NGO volunteered to go to certain villages and teach children about things which are not emphasized upon in the government schools.

Another such programme was conducted a month later, on 2nd January 2022, where children from different places were taught things in many ways i.e, theoretical and practical knowledge and were provided with proper meals.

A recent awareness programme that was held on 24th march, was about spreading awareness of health of the children and women. Women were given proper information about their personal hygiene and how they can keep their children away from fatal diseases as well.

This photograph is from the self defence workshop , conducted in February for women and girls of all age groups. They were educated about the good and bad touch and were given training on how they can protect themselves from unavoidable and dangerous situations.

This photo is related to the awareness programme conducted on 24th march. After the talks by the volunteers, infants and children of the age group 1-3 were given routine checkups and vacinations by the doctors.

This photograph was taken when a similar education drive was held in the northeast side, providing education and meals.
